  • Getting Online

      NOTE: This game requires extensive storage space to use, and the full client can take anywhere from 1GB or more on your hard drive. Please ensure your system specifications are strong enough to support the server.


      1.       To get started, head over to our Downloads Page and get the installer downloaded. This installer is of our own design, completely written and designed by Krona’s team. You’re free to run virus scans and anything else that will make you feel comfortable installing.

      2.       Make sure your system is up to the standard. You’ll find a “recommended” system specifications table on the download page. Compare it to your own to determine if your PC is strong enough to support Krona.

      3.       Create your account on Krona by Registering please use all real information. This helps us, help you, in the case you lose your password or your account is stolen.

      4.       At this point, run the installer and please allow it to install wherever you feel comfortable. You can install it in any spot on your computer, if you have a separated hard drive, we recommend installing it there to avoid cluttering space on your main drive.

      5.       After the installer is done, allow it to run the patcher, and begin patching the remaining files. This is generally an extensive amount of files and will require a great amount of time depending on your connection.

      6.       As soon as it is done patching, you should be free to launch the client and connect to the server. Use the account you created in Step #3 to access Krona’s online services.

      7.       Now that you’re in-game, go ahead and explore the server. You can also take a look into your inventory and see the items that Krona gives to you from the beginning. You’re given a mount, a pet, and some other things. Use them, and explore Krona.

      8.       After you get done messing around in-game, feel free to come join the discussion for bettering Krona happening on the Forums, please feel free to help us out with your wonderful opinions!

      9.       Enjoy using Krona, never stop suggesting new things, and bring all of your friends! If you ever run into any issues, you’re free to look throughout the forums for answers, as well as contact any of the team members that you find in the following section.

  • Securing Your Account

      1.       Simple Naming Advice

      a.       One of the largest issues modern MMO players experience is the naming crisis. This is basically the occurrence of using the same or similar names on every single server/service they use, which can lead to previous server’s corrupt owners/team members having access to your old passwords. As a general note, it is not advised that you use the same naming behavior on each service you use. Try something new on Krona, see what you can think of.

      2.       Creating a Secure Password

      a.       Another one of the issues that players encounter is a lack of password strength. You will want to note that passwords are the only defense you’ve got from someone else gaining access to the account that you’ve put good time and money into. If you want to prevent someone from gaining access, please be smart with the choosing of your password. As a general note, your password should contain all three of the following things: numbers, letters, uppercase and lowercase characters.

      b.      Tip: If you feel your password is too complex to remember, create a text file under a secure name, in a secure folder, and store your passwords.

      c.       Tip: Change your password every 2-3 weeks to prevent having the same one. This will ensure if someone has access to your account, it isn’t for long.

      d.      Tip: Team members will NEVER ask you to give them your password for literally any reason whatsoever.

      3.       Sharing Policies

      a.       You are liable for your own account, and your assets. If you give your account information to someone, then you are giving them complete control of your assets. This is your decision, and will not be supported by the team in the case of theft of any items or services on Krona, please be sure to keep your assets in only the hands of your most trusted friends. Again, Krona will not reimburse any items that are lost from willingly giving your information away for any reason.

      4.       Inputting a Valid Email

      a.       One of the easiest things you can do to secure your account is provide a valid e-mail during the registration process. If you successfully do this, we can verify your identity by sending emails. This speeds up the process ten-fold and allows us to help you that much more quick. This is a vital thing to do to protect your account!


  • How to Donate

      Krona is run off support, plain and simple; both monetary and helpfulness! We thrive off the help of our faithful supporters, and as such, we provide a system of reward for those that do donate to us. You can find the information needed in the section below.


      1.       Where Do Donations Go

      a.       A players donations will always go towards bettering the server. We are constantly looking at finding new, stable, enterprise-level hosting platforms to better the in-game speed of Krona. Along with that, we’re constantly working on finding and funding new projects, such things you’ll see have come along are things like this website. Donations are used to better Krona, and to give a better experience to everyone involved.

      2.       Refunds

      a.       We do not cater to refunds here, so please remain diligent when donating. Your donation will go through the paypal system in such a way that refunding your payment will result in a large dispute process that will freeze the money in question. Should this occur: we will first ban all of your access to Krona’s services; alongside that, we will also terminate any contact you’ve got to Krona, this includes the discussion boards. You will never be welcomed back.

      3.       How To Use Your Crowns

      a.       Spend these wisely! There a multitude of ways to use your crowns. You can sell them in-game to players, since there is always a steady economy of crowns in-game. Alongside that, the item mall is open for business! You can find a multitude of items and fashions to help you get started on Krona in the Item Mall. Enjoy shopping!


  • How to Vote

We’ve got a great API system running with Gtop, which allows us to track the votes as well as determine based on IPs the status of your voting period (how long you’ve got till you can vote again). It’s very simple to vote.


      1.       What Does Voting Do

      a.       We do our very best to stay competitive with the servers around us. They always promise something new, something exciting, but provide the same basic service as every other server before them. On Krona, we tried to do something new, offer something that hasn’t ever been seen before. If you vote, it means you support us with this conquest, it means you support us with the mission we have here. It shows you support us, and it inspires others to support us too.

      2.       What Can I Spend My Voting Points On

      a.       As of now, there’s plenty of variety that can be chosen from. We’ve got an entire section of the Item Mall exclusive to voting materials, along with an ultra-exclusive stated set waiting for those that wish to vote as frequently as possible. Keep in mind, this is something that we consider vitally important to the server. If you’ve got items of things for the voting mall, put those thoughts into the discussion boards! We've implemented an experience scroll into the Voting Mall too that doubles your in-game experience. Grab it to help with leveling!
